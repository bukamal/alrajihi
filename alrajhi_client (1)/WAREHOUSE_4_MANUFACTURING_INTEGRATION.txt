Warehouse-4: Manufacturing Warehouse Integration

- Added raw_warehouse_id and output_warehouse_id to production_orders.
- ProductionOrderDialog now selects raw-material and finished-goods warehouses.
- Material availability checks use the raw-material warehouse.
- Production consumption records warehouse OUT movements.
- Production completion records warehouse IN movements.
- Production reverse checks finished-goods warehouse and reverses both warehouse sides.
- BOM remains warehouse-neutral. Warehouses are chosen at production order level.
